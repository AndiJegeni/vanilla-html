Our story

Founded 2019 · Brooklyn & Lisbon · Independent since day one

Halcyon began in 2019 as two designers who'd spent a decade at larger agencies and tired of the churn. We wanted a studio where research wasn't a line item, where engineers sat next to designers, and where a brand could actually ship.

Today we're a team of seven across New York and Lisbon. We work with a handful of clients each quarter — early-stage founders who need a full brand from scratch, growth-stage teams rebuilding their product, and the occasional nonprofit that catches our eye.

We believe the best work comes from long, uninterrupted focus. We don't run pitches. We don't chase trends. We only take on projects where we think we can do something genuinely good.

What we believe

Four principles that shape every engagement, from first call to handoff.

i.

The details are the work.

We sweat kerning, empty states, and loading spinners. The small things compound into the feeling of the whole product.

ii.

No decks full of fluff.

We show work in progress. We push back when we disagree. We'd rather have one hard conversation than ten polite ones.

iii.

Fewer projects, done properly.

We cap our roster intentionally. One team, one project, one deep engagement at a time. That's how we ship work we're proud of.

iv.

Craft outlives trend.

We design for the long arc of a brand or product. We build things meant to age well, not peak on launch day.
