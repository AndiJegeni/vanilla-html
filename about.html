About the studio

A small studio doing careful work for people who care about the details.

We started Kilo in 2019 because we wanted to work on fewer things, more deeply, with people we actually like.

Our story

Kilo was founded by two designers who had spent a decade at larger agencies and product teams and grown tired of the churn. We wanted a studio where research wasn't a line item, where engineers sat next to designers, and where a brand could actually ship.

Today we're a team of seven across New York and Lisbon. We work with a handful of clients each quarter — early-stage founders who need a full brand from scratch, growth-stage teams rebuilding their product, and the occasional nonprofit that catches our eye.

We believe the best work comes from long, uninterrupted focus. We don't run pitches, we don't chase trends, and we only take on projects where we think we can do something genuinely good.

What we believe

Four principles that shape every project.

01 — Craft

The details are the work.

We sweat kerning, empty states, and loading spinners. The small things compound into the feeling of the whole product.

02 — Honesty

No decks full of fluff.

We show work in progress. We push back when we disagree. We'd rather have one hard conversation than ten polite ones.

03 — Focus

Fewer projects, done properly.

We cap our roster intentionally. One team, one project, one deep engagement at a time. That's how we ship work we're proud of.
