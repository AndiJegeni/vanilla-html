About

The story behind the shapes.

This site began as a single page of shapes. Circles, squares, triangles. Nothing more than a study of form against a plain white background.

Over time it grew, as small things do, into something with a few more rooms. An about page. A gallery. A place to look around.

The philosophy has not changed. Black ink on white paper. Clean edges. No ornament that does not earn its place.
