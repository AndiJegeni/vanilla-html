Frequently asked

A few things people usually ask before we start working together.

How long does a typical engagement last? +

Brand projects run 8–12 weeks. Product engagements usually start at 3 months and can extend to a year. Marketing sites are typically 6–10 weeks. We'll give you a clear scope and timeline before signing, and we don't start work without one.

Do you take on very early-stage startups? +

Sometimes, yes. If you're pre-launch and know exactly what you need, we can often do a focused engagement. Get in touch and we'll talk through fit. We especially love first-brand and first-product engagements when the founding team is clear about what they're making.

Can you work with our in-house design team? +

Often the best projects are collaborations with an internal team. We're happy to embed, lead, or work alongside whatever structure you already have — we'll figure out the right cadence together in the first week.

Do you offer ongoing retainers? +

Yes, for a small number of long-term clients. We reserve one or two retainer slots each year, typically for teams we've worked with before or close referrals. Reach out — we'll tell you honestly if there's availability.

What does a typical engagement cost? +

Brand projects start at $25k and run up to $150k depending on scope. Product and ongoing work is quoted per engagement. We're happy to share detailed proposals once we've talked through what you actually need — no templates, no surprise line items.
