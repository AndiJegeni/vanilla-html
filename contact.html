Frequently asked

A few things people usually ask.

How long does a typical engagement last?

Brand projects run 8–12 weeks. Product engagements usually start at 3 months and can extend to a year. We'll give you a clear scope and timeline before signing.

Do you take on very early-stage startups?

Sometimes, yes. If you're pre-launch and know exactly what you need, we can often do a focused engagement. Get in touch and we'll talk through fit.

Can you work with our in-house design team?

Often the best projects are collaborations with an internal team. We're happy to embed, lead, or work alongside whatever structure you already have.

Do you offer retainers?

Yes, for a small number of long-term clients. We reserve one or two retainer slots each year for teams we've worked with before.
